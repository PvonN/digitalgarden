## Philipp von Neumann {#philipp-von-neumann}

Composer &amp; Performer of Electronic Music
Based in Essen, Germany

**About**

I create contemporary electronic music based on digital sound synthesis, algorithmic structures, and open-ended processes.
My concept of **Permacomposing** understands composition as an evolving, sustainable engagement with tools, materials, and mental states.

**Current Focus**

-   🔬 Chaotic systems in sound synthesis
-   🧬 Algorithmic Composition and Generative Systems
-   🛠 Open-source tools for composition and structure

**Upcoming Activities**

-   🗓 2025-10-25 – Soundinstallation @ **NOW! Festival**, Essen
-   🗓 2025-06-08 – Premiere of new work for **Harp and Electronics** @ Folkwang UdK, Essen
