## Overview {#overview}

vgplot is a [Common Lisp]({{< relref "20230813193858-lisp.md" >}}) librarie, which works as wrapper for [Gnuplot]({{< relref "20250401140406-gnuplot.md" >}}). It allows the plotting of data similiar to [Matlab]({{< relref "20250401141345-matlab.md" >}}).
