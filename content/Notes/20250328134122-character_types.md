## Overview {#overview}

One can classify characters as different types. Here a small overview.


## Content {#content}

| Type         | Description                                      | Example             |
|--------------|--------------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| numeric      | digits                                           | 0 - 9               |
| alpha        | alphabetic characters                            | a - z, A - Z        |
| alphanumeric | combination of numeric and alphabetic characters | 0 - 0, a - z, A - Z |
| uppercase    | uppercase alphabetic characters                  | A - Z               |
| lowercase    | lowercase alphabetic characters                  | a - z               |
