## philipp von neumann {#philipp-von-neumann}

\_composer | \_performer | \_developer

Philipp von Neumann is a composer and performer of contemporary
electronic music based in Essen, Germany.

Von Neumann explores fundamental techniques of digital sound
synthesis, algorithmic structures, and an experimental approach to
musical form. His works delve into themes such as collective mental
exhaustion, utopian states of being, and striving towards acceptance
as a mechanism of decision-making.

He does not consider his compositions as finished pieces but rather as
snapshots of his current practice. His creative process
is the artwork itself, which he describes as "Permacomposing"-a
concept he developed by drawing ideas from permaculture, cybernetics,
and maintenance art. Permacomposing embodies a conscious approach to
materials and tools, a sustainable engagement with one’s mental
capacities, and a networked, diverse mode of working.

As an advocate of the open-source movement, von Neumann develops his
own tools and software and shares them with the public. He
also writes and publishes texts on topics such as Permacomposing,
sound synthesis, and computer music.


## short bio {#short-bio}

-   **1989** – Born in Essen, Germany as Philipp Neumann

-   **Research focus:** Chaotic synthesis &amp; [Algorithmic Composition]({{< relref "20250119143902-algorithmische_komposition.md" >}})
-   **Active as** performer, composer for dance, programmer for interactive

    algorithmic composition
